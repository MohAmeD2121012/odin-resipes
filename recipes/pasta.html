Ground Beef Pasta Recipe

This Ground Beef Pasta recipe is an easy 30 minute meal that you can make with any kind of pasta. Your family will love the creamy, cheesy, Italian sauce!

Ingredients

  • 700g ground beef
    • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 Tablespoons flour, optional-helps thicken the sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • two cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup half and half, (half cream, half milk)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 250g pasta

Step-by-step : Ground Beef Pasta

  • Cook and crumble the ground beef and onions. Drain grease. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add salt, Worcestershire sauce, and flour. Stir to combine and cook for 3 minutes. Add chicken broth, tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Bring a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  • Cook for 15 minutes while you start boiling water for the pasta. Boil the pasta for 7 minutes or per package instructions.
  • Slowly stir in the half and half to the sauce. Reduce heat to low and sprinkle in the cheese. Stir to combine.
  • Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.

Instructions

  1. Shred the cheese and set it aside along with the half and half. We don’t want those to be cold when we add them to the sauce.
  2. Cook and crumble the ground beef over medium heat. Add the onions halfway through and cook until softened.
  3. Drain grease. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add salt, Worcestershire sauce, and flour. Stir to combine and cook for 3 minutes to remove raw flour taste.
  5. Add chicken broth and use a silicone spatula to clean the bottom of the pan. Add the tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Bring a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cook for 15 minutes while you start boiling water for the pasta. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and cook for 7 minutes or per package instructions.
  6. Slowly add in the half and half to the sauce, stirring as you go.
  7. Reduce heat to low and slowly sprinkle in the cheese. Stir to combine. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce

Enjoy :)
